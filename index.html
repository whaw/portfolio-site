About Me

William has a wide view on the web development process. As a young lad he left Vancouver with a Diploma in Advanced Web Content Development for the bright lights of Toronto. There he built on his understanding of how things work with courses in .NET applications development (but that was a long time ago), 13 years of industry experience as a front end developer, and further rounding things out with project coordinating, quality assurance and frontline client support.

Driven to produce better web content for all, he has helped companies such as Anderson DDB, Transcontinental, Marsh STARS and many more meet their web objectives, while earning the appreciation of happy clients and a few awards along the way.

And when he's not living in digital space, he can be found in the great outdoors. He loves the outdoors. Like, loves loves the outdoors, and MEC.
