A closer look

Purpose

To demonstrate a level of professionalism, polish and experience, with an eye and appreciation for design and focus on customer experience. Moreover, to provide insight into interests and personality as fit with culture, values and team being key to a successful addition.

Build stack

  • SCSS
  • HTML/Bootstrap
  • JavaScript
  • NPM
  • Gulp
  • Babel

Why Bootstrap?

Speed to market. I have years of experience hand coding layouts and designs, debugging and I am up to date in the latest tools, but as often is the case, iterating and prototyping quickly was also the fit here so leveraging a framework was the most expedient and efficient way to see results and meet objectives.

Approach

Everything is broken down into modules and reusable parts. JavaScript is written with objects in mind and deployment versions are built from automated tasks, that compile, convert and optimize assets.

Hero image is loaded progressively. Image qualities did not permit for 2x versions. Code validates against W3C standards and tests consistently across browsers and devices.

William has a wide view on the web development process.

As a young lad he left Vancouver, with a Diploma in Advanced Web Content Development in hand, for the bright lights of Toronto. There he built on his understanding of how things work with courses in .NET applications development (but that was a long time ago), 17 years of industry experience as a front end developer, and rounding things out with project coordinating, quality assurance and frontline client support.

Driven to produce better web content for all, he has helped companies such as Anderson DDB, Transcontinental, Marsh STARS and many more meet their web objectives, while earning the appreciation of happy clients and a few awards along the way.

And when he's not in digital space, he can be found in the outdoors. He loves the outdoors. Like, loves loves the outdoors, and MEC.
